About me:

I am a fourth-year undergraduate at the University of California at Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering/Computer Sciences and Applied Mathematics. My advisor is Professor James Demmel. I am currently applying to PhD programs in computer science. My research interests include:

  • High-performance and parallel computing
  • Scientific computing
  • Numerical linear algebra
  • Data mining, cloud computing, and distributed systems
This past summer I was a software engineering intern at Google on the Google Chrome team working on Native Client and naclports. You can check out a Chrome Web Store app I helped create as part of my internship here.

Some of my work (including this web site) is on github.
